Books Written

Handbook of Artificial Intelligence and Wearables: Applications and Case Studies

The ever-changing world of wearable technologies makes it difficult for experts and practitioners to keep up with the most recent developments. This handbook provides a solid understanding of the significant role that AI plays in the design and development of wearable technologies along with applications and case studies.

A Graph Theoretic Approach to Heterogeneous Data Clustering

Data clustering is the process of automatically grouping data objects into different groups (clusters). The contribution of this book is threefold: homogeneous clustering of images, pairwise heterogeneous data co-clustering, and high-order star-structured heterogeneous data co-clustering. First, we propose a semantic-based hierarchical image clustering framework based on multi-user feedback.

Artificial Intelligence and Knowledge Processing: Third International Conference, AIKP 2023, Hyderabad, India, October 6–8, 2023, Revised Selected Papers ... Computer and Information Science Book 2127

This book constitutes the Revised Selected Papers of the Third International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Knowledge Processing, AIKP 2023, held in Hyderabad, India, during October 6–8, 2023. The 20 full papers and 8 short papers were carefully selected from 118 submissions. The research areas include: Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning; Deep Learning and Computer Vision; Natural Language Processing; Intelligent Control.
